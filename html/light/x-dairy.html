Module 01

Milk Parlor Management

Milk Parlor Management helps dairy farms efficiently manage daily milk collection and production processes. Farmers can record milk collection data digitally, reducing manual errors and saving time. The module supports better operational efficiency by providing clear visibility into daily production, per-cow yield, and overall farm productivity.

  • Milk AnalyticsTrack daily, weekly, and monthly milk yield per cow and session. Analyze cow performance over time and identify top producers.
  • Call Feed MilkRecord discarded milk from the milking machine directly in the system. Easily track and account for milk that fails quality checks.
  • Collector IDAssign milk collection tasks to individual collectors using the collector ID system. The module ties each milk collection session to a specific employee.
  • Sale MilkLog milk sold directly from the farm to buyers. Track sale volume and generate summaries for financial reporting.
Module 02

Cow Management

Cow Management helps dairy farms efficiently organize and track all cow-related records in one centralized system. Farmers can store important details such as cow ID, breed, age, health status, and production stage. Cow Analytics provides useful insights into herd performance by analyzing milk production levels and overall health trends.

  • Cow AnalyticsView detailed performance data for every cow in the herd, including milk yield, health history, and reproductive status.
  • Manage CowAdd, update, or retire cow records with full profile details including breed, age, weight, and production data.
  • Manage GroupOrganize cows into groups based on categories like age, breed, or production stage for easier group monitoring.
  • Animal ProfileEach cow has a complete profile with a full record including ID, breed, age, production level, and health history.
  • Cow SaleLog and manage the sale of cows directly within the system. Track sale price, buyer details, and finalize the transaction.

Module 04

Health Management

Health Management is a comprehensive module designed to help dairy farmers efficiently monitor and manage the health of each animal in the herd. It provides a systematic approach to recording health events, including routine check-ups, symptoms, illnesses, diagnoses, and recovery programs.

  • Health AnalyticsGet a complete health overview of your herd with real-time data on disease outbreaks, treatment rates, and recovery trends.
  • TreatmentsLog every treatment administered to your cows, including medications, dosages, and the veterinarian who provided care.
  • Vaccine MonitorSchedule and track vaccinations for each cow with automated reminders to ensure herd immunity stays up to date.
  • Lab CertificationsStore and manage all laboratory test results and certifications linked to individual animals for compliance records.
  • Hoof TrimmingSchedule and track hoof trimming events to prevent lameness, with records of trimmer, date, and cow condition.
  • Death RecordsDocument any animal deaths including cause, date, and associated costs for accurate herd accounting and reporting.
Module 03

Reproduction Management

Reproduction Management is a comprehensive module designed to help dairy farmers efficiently oversee and maintain the health of their herd through careful monitoring of all reproductive activities. The system supports breeding program planning, conception tracking, and farm analytics to optimize reproductive efficiency.

  • Reproduction AnalyticsAccess detailed reproduction reports and statistics including conception rates, calving intervals, and breeding success.
  • Heat DetectorLog heat detection events and cycle tracking for each cow to identify the optimal breeding window and maximize conception rates.
  • Artificial InseminationRecord all AI procedures including semen type, technician, date, and outcomes for full reproductive history per cow.
  • Pregnancy DiagnosisTrack pregnancy confirmation results and expected calving dates to plan herd management and resource allocation.
  • Calving ManagementRecord calving events with details on the calf, birth complications, and post-calving cow recovery for complete records.
  • Dry-Off ManagementPlan and manage the dry-off period for each cow to ensure proper preparation for the next lactation cycle.
